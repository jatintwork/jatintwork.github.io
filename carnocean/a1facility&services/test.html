About Us

Cruise And Cargo Management India Pvt. Ltd.

SkyHaamburg.com is the career portal of Cruise And Cargo Management India Pvt. Ltd.

Sky Haamburg is India's largest manning firm providing highly skilled and trained personnel for cruise ship and Shipyard operations worldwide. Presently, we administer in excess of 21000 Indian personnel and many cruise ships and shipyard.

This means that we bring to India the best Cruise ship and Shipyard jobs in the world. Our recruitment procedures are fair and transparent without any bias whatsoever. We are duly licensed by the DG Shipping office in India to carry out recruiting and placement services in India.
