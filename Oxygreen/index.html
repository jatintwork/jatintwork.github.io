About Us

Our Services are

Oxygreen Hi-Tech India has been working successfully in the field of solid waste management in Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, Thane, and Pune. We collect waste from Residential Societies, Hotels, Malls, colleges, and Commercial Corporates Park and Segregate it, in a very useful way, wet waste is used to make compost manure and dry waste is used for recycling. We use Hi-tech machines for processing of wet waste.

Managing waste properly, is essential for building sustainable and livable cities, but it remains a challenge for many cities. These practices create serious health, safety, and environmental consequences. Poorly managed waste serves as a breeding ground for disease vectors, contributes to global climate change, and can even promote urban violence.
